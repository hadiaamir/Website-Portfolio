Description

An individual assignment was assigned, where we had to design the school's yearbook cover for the year of 2016 - 2017. The key theme we had to follow that year was "Vision". Every little detail in this cover design was completely from strach. At first, multiple PSD files were created for the eye, river, buildings, etc. Then merged into the final product to decrease file size and easier movement of modules. My design was created through the use of Adobe Photoshop and was selected after many rounds of voting to be represented and viewed by 1000+ students.

Date: Dec 2016 - Jan 2017
