Description

The term “hypebeast” - a person who follows a trend to be cool or in style (bustle.com), has been used very frequently throughout the current young generation. Keeping up with the lastest fashion, sneakers, music and sports is a must for the youth and for me. My objective for this website was to provide the latest “hype” or “trending” fashion, sneakers, music and sports feeds.
